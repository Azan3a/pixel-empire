# Natural Features: Rivers & Lakes

Detailed specifications for the primary waterways connecting the mainland zones.

## 1. The River (Central Waterway)

The main river flows from the **Mountains (Zone 2)** down through the **Forest (Zone 1)**, **Old Town (Zone 3)**, and eventually empties into the **Harbor (Zone 4)**.

- **Average Width:** 40-60 pixels.
- **Flow Rate:** Constant southward flow animation.
- **Interactions:** Fishing (Freshwater), Drinking (Energy), Swimming (not recommended).
- **Bridges:**
  - **Zone 1 Bridge:** (1200, 4500) - Old wooden bridge.
  - **Zone 3 Bridge:** (3800, 4800) - Main stone bridge.
